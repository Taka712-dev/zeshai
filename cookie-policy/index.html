Zesh Cookie policy

Introduction

Zesh.ai uses cookies to improve your experience when you visit our website. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control your cookie preferences.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device by a website that you visit. They are used to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences, so that you don’t have to enter them again the next time you visit the site.

What types of cookies do we use?

Zesh.ai uses the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of our website and cannot be turned off. They are used to provide basic functionality, such as secure log-in and adjusting your consent preferences.

    ID: cookieyes-consent
    DOMAIN: zesh.ai
    DURATION: 1 year
    CookieYes sets this cookie to remember users’ consent preferences so that their preferences are respected on subsequent visits to this site. It does not collect or store any personal information about the site visitors.

    ID: __cf_bm
    DOMAIN: .vimeo.com
    DURATION: 1 hour
    This cookie, set by Cloudflare, is used to support Cloudflare Bot Management.

  • Analytics cookies: These cookies help us to understand how visitors interact with our website. This information is used to improve our website and provide a better user experience.

    ID: vuid
    DOMAIN: .vimeo.com
    DURATION: 1 year 1 month 4 days
    Vimeo installs this cookie to collect tracking information by setting a unique ID to embed videos on the website.

How can I control my cookie preferences?

You can control your cookie preferences by using the cookie consent tool at the bottom of our website. You can also control your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to our Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website.

Contact us
