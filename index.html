Powering a Sustainable and Deflationary Ecosystem

Built for growth, driven by innovation, $ZAI fuels the Zesh AI ecosystem through dynamic monetization, deflationary mechanisms, and tiered staking rewards.

Access to Premium Features

Stakers of $ZAI gain access to premium features and services within the ZESH ecosystem. Staking unlocks advanced tools and capabilities to support their Web 3 projects.

Payment and Transaction Discounts

$ZAI facilitates seamless payments and transactions within the ZESH ecosystem. All core ecosystem elements and features within the ZESH platform will offer discounts if the payment is made in $ZAI.

ZESH AI Layer DAO

ZZESH is not a DAO yet, but aims to become one. In the meantime. $ZAI stakers can participate in voting on platform features proposals through the tool HODLers Voice. This tool will help transition towards a DAO, and it helps the community shape the future of the ecosystem. 

Staking Rewards

Staking $ZAI will also grant the possibility for users to be rewarded for this action with certain non-inflationary rewards in the form of further digital tokens.

$ZAI Will be Deflationary

The ZESH platform will from time to time use its available treasury to purchase from secondary markets $ZAI tokens and burn them, thus putting deflationary pressure on the token.

$ZAI Staker Loyalty Program

The most loyal $ZAI stakers will receive enhanced rewards based on the duration and quantity of their stakes.
